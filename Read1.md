## Markdown Syntax
Headers      | Syntax
------------ | ------------
(#) | The largest heading
(##) | The second largest heading
(######) | The smallest heading


Emphasis     | Syntax
------------ | ------------
** | *This text will be italic*
_ _ | This will also be italic
_ txt_ *txt* _ | _You **can** combine them_

Links        | Syntax
------------ | ------------
[the name](the lik) | [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/)
